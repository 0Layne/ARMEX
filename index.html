Protection Against Riots & Intruders

Worried about riots, looting, or smash-and-grab break-ins? Our security film reinforces your existing glass, making it far more difficult for intruders to break through. With fast and easy installation, your windows become a powerful barrier, keeping your property secure 24/7 and giving you peace of mind.

Weather Resistance

Severe weather can cause devastating damage when glass shatters. Our film is engineered to hold glass together during hurricanes, tornados, monsoons, blizzards, or high winds. By keeping windows intact, you reduce the risk of further property damage and ensure greater safety inside.

UV Protection & Energy Efficiency

ARMEX SHIELD security film does more than strengthen glass—it helps protect your interiors and lower energy costs. By blocking harmful UV rays, our film prevents fading of furniture, carpets, and drapes while also reducing heat buildup. Pair it with our tint or decorative films for added privacy, comfort, and style.
