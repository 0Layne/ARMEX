Security Film & Shatter Defense

Strengthen your glass with invisible protection — built to stop intruders, weather, and time.

Security film is one of the most effective ways to reinforce vulnerable glass. By bonding directly to the window, it holds shattered glass in place, making it significantly harder for intruders to gain entry. This layer of protection gives homeowners and businesses peace of mind, knowing their property and people are safer against break-ins, vandalism, or unexpected riots.

At ARMEX SHIELD, we install industry-leading film for homes, businesses, and storefronts — designed to withstand break-ins, extreme weather, and harmful UV rays, all while keeping a professional, clean look.

  • Reinforces glass against forced entry
  • Minimizes injuries from shattered glass
  • Shields interiors from UV damage
  • Maintains a clear, polished appearance

Our state-of-the-art security film is one of the thickest and strongest on the market, designed to transform standard glass into a powerful barrier. We offer two levels of protection: reduce entry, providing 5–10 minutes of resistance, and Anti-Smash & Grab, offering 15–20 minutes of defense against forced entry.
